Life Sciences Packages in the Futureverse Ecosystem

[!NOTE] This report details CRAN and Bioconductor packages within the life sciences (bioinformatics, genomics, clinical research, epidemiology, pharmacology, ecology, etc.) that leverage the Futureverse parallel processing ecosystem. It covers First-Generation Dependencies (packages directly importing or suggesting Futureverse tools) and Second-Generation Dependencies (packages that build on top of those first-generation life science libraries, showcasing transitive ecosystem impact).

Executive Summary

Generation Total Packages Bioconductor CRAN Description
1st Generation 167 32 135 Direct reverse dependencies of Futureverse core packages
2nd Generation 306 132 174 Transitive dependents (depend on 1st-gen life science packages)
Ecosystem Impact 473 164 309 Total combined unique packages influenced
